What it does

Everything a nomad
actually needs.

Built for nomads, by nomads.

Visa countdown dashboard

Your active stop front and center. Animated countdown with entry and exit dates, a progress arc, and urgency colors as your deadline approaches.

Journey timeline & calendar

Every stop in one scrollable timeline. Switch to calendar view to see your movement month by month — past, active, and upcoming stops color-coded.

Smart expiry alerts

Push notifications at 30, 14, 7, and 3 days before your visa expires. You'll never get that sinking airport feeling again.

Visa intelligence planner

Search destinations by passport. Filter by visa-free, VOA, e-Visa, or required. Sort by flight time. Compare up to three countries side by side.

Schengen 90/180 monitor

Rolling 180-day window tracked automatically. Run what-if simulations before you book. Know exactly when you can enter and when you must leave.

Tax residency tracker

Country-specific thresholds, not a flat 183 days. Thailand's 180, Malaysia's 182, Cyprus's 60-day non-dom rule — tracked correctly per country.

Shareable travel cards

Generate clean, brandable cards for every stop. Country landscape backgrounds, your stats, your journey. Ready for stories, posts, or the group chat.

CSV & PDF export

Export your entire journey history as a spreadsheet or PDF. Useful for visa applications, tax accountants, or just keeping your own records clean.

Home screen widget

Your countdown, visible without opening the app. Days remaining, active country, urgency state — right on your lock screen or home screen.
